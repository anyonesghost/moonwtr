## Overview

Signal Terminal is a browser-based text adventure built entirely in vanilla JS. Players navigate an abandoned signal relay station through typed commands, uncovering four distinct endings depending on the choices they make.

## Scope

- Narrative design & branching script
- Vanilla JS engine (no framework)
- Ambient audio design
- localStorage save system
- Web deployment
