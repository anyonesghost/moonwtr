## Overview

SIGNAL: RISING is a multi-episode video series that collapses the fiction of the Marathon universe into real-world footage. A Marathon-liveried Mini Cooper S serves as a roaming MIDA propaganda unit — appearing at locations, events, and ordinary streets as though the lore had leaked out into Ohio.

- Series concept & worldbuilding
- Cinematography & direction
- Motion graphics & titles
- Sound design
- Multi-platform distribution
